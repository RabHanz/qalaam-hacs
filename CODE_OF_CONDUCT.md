# Code of Conduct

Qalaam serves a global Muslim community across every culture, language, and madhab. Contributors of every background are welcome; the only conduct standard is one that any thoughtful community runs on:

- **Be respectful.** Disagree with ideas, never with people.
- **Assume good faith.** When something seems off, ask before accusing.
- **Honor adab.** Avoid commentary on personal religious practice or sectarian disputes in project channels — Qalaam is shared infrastructure, not a forum.
- **Give credit.** Cite sources, ADRs, prior work.
- **Stay focused.** Off-topic political or polemical content is out of scope.

Maintainers reserve the right to remove content or restrict participation that violates these norms.

For private concerns: contact the maintainers via `SECURITY.md`'s confidential channel.
